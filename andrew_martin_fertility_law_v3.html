The Invisible Profit Killers

The most damaging costs in Andrew's practice are not the big-ticket legal matters — they're the 5-minute and 15-minute tasks that repeat dozens of times each day. They don't feel expensive in the moment, but compounded across a week, a month, a year, they add up to an extraordinary drain on a specialist attorney's most valuable resource: focused time.

  • Consent form uploads: 4 minutes per form × 20 forms per day × 250 working days = 333 hours per year spent on pure data entry. At R2,000/hr, that's R666,000 in attorney time doing what a system should automate.
  • SigningHub mandate setup: 15 minutes per document instead of 1. If Andrew processes 5 mandates per day, the avoidable overhead is 70 minutes daily — 292 hours per year — worth R584,000.
  • Frozen material agreements: R1,750 per agreement, 15 agreed-to in February alone, zero followed through. Not because clients refused — because there was no follow-up system. That's R26,250 evaporating from a single month without a single outbound message.
  • Email triage: 40–50 emails per day from three different client types (new leads, active journeys, clinics) all landing in one inbox with no priority filter — causing the inbox to balloon to 120 while Andrew runs consultations.
"If I had something doing what could be automated in the background, I could structure my days right — two hours of emails, two hours of consultations either side of lunch, three hours for drafting or checking AI's draft. I would be the most productive human being on earth." — Andrew Martin

How the R4.7M Figure Was Built

The headline "cost of doing nothing" is built from three traceable components. Every line item is sourced from the discovery transcript or Andrew's own estimates:

  • Andrew's principal time overhead (R3.12–3.64M/yr) — 30–35 hrs/week of automatable admin × R2,000/hr × 52 weeks. Draws from PP1 (15 hrs), PP4 (6 hrs), PP5 (5 hrs), PP6 (5 hrs), PP7 (3 hrs). R2,000/hr applied as a conservative assumption.
  • Frozen material agreement revenue (R1.2M/yr) — Andrew's own direct projection of R100,000/month. Draws from PP3. Highest confidence figure in the report.
  • Support staff automatable time (R195K/yr) — 30 hrs/week combined staff time on routine queries × R125/hr × 52 weeks. Draws from PP9. ⚠ Hours estimate requires validation.

Total: R3.12M + R1.2M + R195K = ~R4.5M, rounded to R4.7M at midpoint of principal time range.

⚠ Figures to confirm in the scoping session: daily mandate volume (5/day assumed), staff query time (2 hrs/person/day assumed), and lead recapture rate (10–15% assumed). R2,000/hr is applied as a conservative assumption and does not require validation.
